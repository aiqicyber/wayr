Create!

What is this dang website?

This website lets you create your own personal psychology profile by answering guided questions and reflections. You can save your profile on your own device, edit it over time, and export it as a file. Later, you can re-import older profiles to compare them with your current one and see how your thoughts, moods, or perspectives may have changed. The site also provides space for journaling and guided reflections, as well as links to trusted mental health resources for extra support.

Whoa, wait. Why should I give you my data?

Your data is never sent to any server or stored online. Everything you write stays inside your browser using secure local storage (IndexedDB). If you want to save your progress, you can export your profile as a file and keep it on your own device. This means only you decide where your information goes and who has access to it. The website does not collect, share, or track any personal details; your reflections remain completely private.
