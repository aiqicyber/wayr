What is this dang website?

This website lets you create your own personal psychology profile card by answering the guided questions and reflections! You can use our comparison tool to compare older profiles with your current one and see how your thoughts, moods, or perspectives may have changed. The site provides space for journaling and guided reflections, as well as links to trusted mental health resources for extra super support.

Whoa, wait. Why should I give you my data?

Your data is never sent to any server or stored online!!!!! Everything you write stays inside your browser using secure local storage (IndexedDB). The website does not collect, share, or track any personal details; your reflections remain completely private, locked by a master password YOU set.
